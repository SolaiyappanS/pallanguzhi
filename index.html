How To Play Pallanguzhi?

You can start the game by clicking the play button or the Space key. Player 1 should start the game.

One can change themselves as the other player by clicking the "You're Player X" button.

The upper half of the board belongs to Player 1 and the lower half belongs to Player 2. Initially, 35 points(5 points for each kuzhi) will be given to both players. And the points will be distributed to all the kuzhis.

The player should choose a kuzhi that belongs to him/her. Then the points in that kuzhi will be distributed to the next kuzhis one by one.

After distributing all the points the player should distribute the points in adjacent kuzhi to the next kuzhis one by one.

(Here these will be done automatically by pressing the Play button or Space key continuously) The turn will end only after there are no points in the adjacent kuzhi. Then the points in the next kuzhi go to the Player who is playing.

Once a player finishes his/her turn, it notifies the other player to choose a kuzhi that belongs to him/her. Repeat this procedure and continue playing.

If a kuzhi reaches the number 4 at the end of a turn, that will be taken and the points will be added to the player to whom the kuzhi belongs. It is called 'PASU'.

If any half of the board becomes completely zero, the round will end. Then the distribution process will be done once again, but this time all the kuzhis of the player having points less than 35 will not be filled. Those empty kuzhis will be 'BLOCKED' and a player should not put any points in that kuzhi.

When a player gets less than five points at the end of a round, the game will come to an end and the player with higher points will be considered the Winner.
